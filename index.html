Privacy Policy

GTM Detective runs entirely in your browser. Your container data never leaves your device.

  • No upload. When you open or drag in a GTM container JSON, it is read and analysed locally by JavaScript in this page. It is never sent to any server, and there is no backend to receive it.
  • No storage. The container is held only in memory for the current session. Nothing is written to disk, cookies, or local storage. Closing or reloading the tab discards it.
  • No tracking. This app sets no analytics, cookies, or trackers, and collects no personal data about you.
  • Third-party assets. The graph library (Cytoscape.js and its dagre layout) is loaded from public CDNs (cloudflare, jsdelivr) the first time you open the app. Those requests are standard file downloads and carry none of your container data. To run fully offline, host these libraries locally instead.
  • Exports. PNG exports are generated in your browser and saved directly to your device — they are not transmitted anywhere.

Because everything is local, you can safely analyse production containers that contain proprietary tracking logic.
